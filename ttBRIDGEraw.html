What is bridge?

Top 5 cards (AKQJ10) = honors. Rest = spots.
Can't follow suit (out) & play diff suit that can't win = discard.
Some deals > trump suit (outranks other suits; played only if out of lead suit) [= 'ruff'].
Defence communicates thru cards they play!
Bidding: Auction (> right to be declarer & pick trump suit; last bid) > contract (> 'level' & 'strain'/denomination):
 1♥ = have at least 5 hearts.
 1 NT = above avg hand w/ = cards ea suit.
Hand evaluation: assess trick-taking potential.

Opening Bid
■ Opening 1 of suit 'promises' 12 High Card Points (HCP):
   A=4 | K=3 | Q=2 | J=1
■ If can open (^) - ? which suit:
● 1) 1♥ or 1♠ promise 5 cards (2/1 = '5-card majors' system).
● 2) open longest suit.
● - val for prtnr to know if have 4 or 5-card major. OPEN of 1♥ or 1♠ = 5 (later = 4).
■ w/ long suits of = lgth [p65]:
● 1) open higher ranked (not stronger).
● 2) w/ 2 3-card minors > ♣ (1♦ open = at least 4 ♦).
■ 1&2NT (prefer over 1 of suit):
● Balanced hand: no sigletons or voids & only 1 doubleton (4333; 4432; 5332).
● 1NT = 15-17 HCP + balanced.
● 2NT = 20-21 HCP + balanced.

Responding to 1 of Suit
■ PRIORITIES:
● Support opener's MAJOR
● Suggest new suit
● Suggest NT if no viable MAJOR
● Support opener's minor
■ Communicate hand strength (req 25 HCP to bid game):
● 6-9 HCP (min)
 - pass w/ less
● 10-12 HCP (invitational)
● 13+ HCP (game forcing)
■ Major suit fit = 8 cards bet 2 hands
- w/ open of 1 ♥ or ♠ = 5 cards > respr can raise w/ 3
● fit w/ 'min' > raise 1
● fit w/ 'inv' > raise 2
● fit w/ 'forcing' > bid new suit 1st
■ Bidding major locates major fit. Bidding minor > ? play NT contract (strgth in q suit).
● Bid new suit (promise 4 cards except 2♥ resp to 1♠ = 5♥) if can't raise opener's major or opener bids minor!
 1-level new = 6+ HCP (min hand).
 2-level (if 1-level unavail) new = 13+ HCP   (game forcing)  ^ but 'bid cheaply'!
2/1 = 5 card majors (15-17 HCP 1 NT open) + game forcing 2-level new suit resp.
■ Bid cheaply: don't 'jump' to 2 just bec have 13+ HCP. w/ mult poss new suits:
● start w/ longest
● w/ 2 equal
  - 5 cards: start higher
  - 4 cards: bid lower
 w/ no 4-card suit to bid & min or inv hand bid 1NT
■ Responders new suit bid is forcing (prtnr must bid again if interv opp passes).
1 NT resp is special:
 † post minor open 1NT = min hnd & is not forcing
 † post major opn 1NT = min or inv & is forcing
