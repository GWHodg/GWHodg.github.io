    connected 1-piece (arms + trunk) ROTATION w/ R elbow in vs chest w/o > fwd or up @ impact.

  2. Bend @ hips, turn w/ SHOULDERS/ribs (spine straight) & keep R arm vs side
    - add a little legs on dwnswg > full F/T ('pose')
  3. Stop/slow fwd body/hand 'action' @ impact > allows/forces R wrist to UN-extend/flex > clubhead-hands 'catch-up' (lag) *
  4. ? Conscious R wist straighten @ impact
